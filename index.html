Your guides to the internet

VEGA is a product born out of The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill Department of Computer Science focused on making computer usage easier and more detailed to beginners. It allows educators to create guides (paths) through webpages, annotating points they see fit and turning static websites into interactive tutorials.
